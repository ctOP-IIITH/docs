# Smart City Living Lab, IIIT-Hyderabad
Smart City Living Lab is a research lab at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-Hyderabad). The lab focuses on research and development in the areas of smart cities, Internet of Things (IoT), and data analytics. SCLL is involved in various projects related to smart cities, including the development of smart city platforms, IoT applications, and data analytics solutions. The lab collaborates with industry partners, government agencies, and academic institutions to address the challenges faced by cities and communities in the digital age. The International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIITH), is home to the Smart City Living Lab where innovative projects like ctOP are developed. This lab focuses on addressing urban challenges through cutting-edge research in ICT, data analytics, and AI. This section provides insights into the lab's objectives, its facilities, and the collaborative environment it fosters.
